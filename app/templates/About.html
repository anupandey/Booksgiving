Everyone has a right to learn

Almost half of India's population drops out of school by the age of 13.

In our country today, the education you receive depends on where you live, what your parents earn. Many guardians are so poor that they cant manage even two square meals a day for their families. It is too much to expect from them to arrange books, note books etc. for their children.On the contrary they find it easy to stop their children's education and put them into some trade to earn money. Thus a large percentage of promising children are denied education. For many impoverished families, Schooling & Buying books is viewed as a financial burden. Poor children have little acess to book at home and in their communities. They attend schools with poorly supported school libraries.


Public funds are insufficient to cover education costs. We believe Education is the most important ingredient for India's success and the one that will determine its growth, and help in its transition from a developing to a developed country. Schools throughout India experience shortages of books.

The government alone can't make improvements as much as it is their job. We all dream of a Beautiful India. Its time we come and act together to change things, help each other and make India truly beautiful.

Together WE can do so much!

Our Approach

We know that it is possible to achieve an India, where every child can attain education.
WE can do this if WE, together act to turn this dream to reality. Happy Booksgiving is founded to help build literacy by putting books into the hands of children who have little or no access to them. Working through organizations that help children in need, We help you connect with those who needs Books.
Happy Booksgiving is proud to work with many wonderful organizations in India to promote literacy and learning. Please show your support for those businesses that host our book collection bins. These businesses help us collect many of the books that we distribute!

Borrowing
